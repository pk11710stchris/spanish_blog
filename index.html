Climate in Ireland

Ireland's climate is temperate and maritime, characterized by mild winters and cool summers. Frequent rainfall, especially along the west coast, contributes to the island's lush vegetation.

Seasons in Ireland

Ireland experiences four distinct seasons: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. Each season brings unique weather conditions, from snowfall in winter to sunny days in summer.
